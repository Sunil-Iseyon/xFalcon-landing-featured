# Legal Pages Editorial Guide

A simple guide for non-technical users to edit legal pages through TinaCMS.

## Quick Start

### Accessing TinaCMS

1. Open your browser and go to: `https://xfalcon.ai/admin`
2. Log in with your TinaCMS credentials
3. You're now in the admin panel

### Finding the Legal Pages

In the left sidebar, you'll see collections. Find and click one of these:
- **Privacy Policy**
- **Terms & Conditions**
- **Security & Trust**

The content will load in the editor.

## Editing Pages

### 1. Basic Information

At the top of the form, you'll see:

- **Title**: Page title (e.g., "Privacy Policy")
- **Description**: Short summary for search engines (2-3 sentences)
- **Last Updated**: Date in format YYYY-MM-DD (e.g., 2024-06-15)

### 2. Sections

The main content is organized into **Sections**. Each section has:

- **ID**: Short name for linking (lowercase, use hyphens) - e.g., `introduction`
- **Title**: Section heading - e.g., "Introduction"
- **Content**: Main text of the section
- **Subsections**: Optional sub-topics within the section

### 3. Adding a New Section

1. Click the **"+ Add" button** next to "Sections"
2. Fill in the fields:
   - **Section ID**: Must be unique (lowercase, hyphens) - e.g., `data-handling`
   - **Title**: What users will see - e.g., "How We Handle Your Data"
   - **Content**: Your text here
3. Click **Save**

### 4. Adding Subsections

Within a section:
1. Click **"+ Add"** next to "Subsections"
2. Add:
   - **Title**: Subsection heading
   - **Content**: Subsection text
3. Click **Save**

### 5. Editing Content

1. Click in the **Content** field
2. Type or paste your text
3. Support for **formatting**:
   - **Bold**: `**bold text**`
   - *Italic*: `*italic text*`
   - Links: `[Link text](https://example.com)`
   - Line breaks: Just press Enter

### 6. Contact Information

Scroll to the **Contact Section** at the bottom:

- **Email**: Contact email (e.g., privacy@xfalcon.ai)
- **Address**: Mailing address (can be multi-line)
- **Title**: Section heading (e.g., "Privacy Team")

### 7. Saving Changes

1. After editing, click the **"Save"** button in the top right
2. Changes are saved to the system
3. They'll be automatically published within minutes

## Formatting Tips

### Writing Best Practices

1. **Clear and Concise**: Use simple, direct language
2. **Short Paragraphs**: Easier to read on mobile
3. **Bullet Points**: Use them for lists
4. **Headings**: Use for organization
5. **Links**: Add to related policies or documents

### Example Content

```
Our security practices include:

- 256-bit encryption for all data
- Multi-factor authentication available
- Regular security audits
- 24/7 monitoring and incident response

For more details, see our Security & Trust page.
```

## Dos and Don'ts

### ✅ DO

- Update the "Last Updated" date when you make changes
- Use clear, simple language
- Link to related sections
- Keep formatting consistent
- Proofread before saving
- Update contact emails if they change

### ❌ DON'T

- Don't use ALL CAPS (hard to read)
- Don't use complex legal jargon without explanation
- Don't leave broken links
- Don't mix different formatting styles
- Don't forget to update the "Last Updated" date
- Don't publish without proofreading

## Common Tasks

### Updating Contact Information

1. Open the page in TinaCMS
2. Scroll to the bottom
3. Find "Contact Section"
4. Update Email or Address
5. Click "Save"

### Changing the Last Updated Date

1. Find the "Last Updated" field at the top
2. Enter today's date in YYYY-MM-DD format
3. Click "Save"

**Format**: 2024-06-15 (Year-Month-Day)

### Adding a New Policy Section

1. Click **"+ Add"** next to "Sections"
2. Enter Section ID (e.g., `new-topic`)
3. Enter Title (e.g., "New Topic")
4. Enter Content
5. Optional: Add Subsections
6. Click "Save"

### Removing a Section

1. Click the section to edit it
2. Look for the **Delete** button (trash icon)
3. Click to remove
4. Click "Save"

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Log In?

- Check username/email is correct
- Reset password if forgotten
- Contact your admin

### Changes Not Appearing?

- Click **Save** button
- Wait 1-2 minutes for deployment
- Refresh the page
- Clear browser cache (Ctrl+Shift+Delete)

### Text Looks Wrong?

- Use plain formatting (no fancy fonts)
- Avoid special characters (use - instead of –)
- Check for extra spaces or line breaks

### Can't Find the Page?

- Look in the left sidebar under "Collections"
- Make sure you're logged in
- Try refreshing the page
